in a large skillet heat 1 tbsp oil over mediumhigh heat  brown chicken all over transfer to crock pot drain any fat from pan heat 1 tbsp oil and fry onion  garlic  coriander  cumin  chili powder  salt and pepper  stirring occasionally until softened  about 6 minute scrape into crock pot add beans and chilies to crock pot and cook on low for 5 hours remove chicken from sauce  cube into bite size pieces and set aside whisk flour with 3 tbsp water and stir into crock pot cover and cook on high for 15 min or until sauce has thickened stir in chicken  fresh cilantro and lime  heat through and serve
wonderful on cold nights. i also make this dish on the stove top simmering for about 1 hour. i like to add 11 12 cup chicken broth to make it a bit more liquidy.
