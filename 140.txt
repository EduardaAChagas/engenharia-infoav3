dissolve yeast in water and honey for about 5 minutes combine honey  olive oil  eggs  and salt in a large mixing bowl slowly add flour and knead until it loosens from sides of the bowl place in large oiled bowl  cover and let rise until about double about one hour  be sure it is in a warm place punch down and remove dough shape dough into rolls about 2 inch in diameter place on an oiled or non stick baking sheet  cover and let rise for 30 minutes preheat oven to 375 degrees bake at about 375 degrees for about 20 minutes or until browned
this recipe turns out great homemade rolls for those who love 100 whole wheat bread.  they rose beautifully and plump in the oven. makes 2025 rolls depending on how big you make them. i made 2 inch dough balls and they rose to 3 inches after baked. to make more rolls just make the dough balls smaller.
