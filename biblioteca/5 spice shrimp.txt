pat shrimp dry and coat them with the 5 spice powder and salt pepper heat oil in a pan at medium high heat add shrimp  cook stirring until shrimp curl and turn pink  12 minutes add the garlic and cook another 30 seconds add the wine and boil until reduced by half and shrimp are opaque  about 1 minute serve warm
this can be done as an appetizer before a meal or as a first course. 5 spice powder is usually found in all grocery stores now. oriental stores would have it and it is good with a number of meats.
