preheat oven to 350f grease an 8 inch square pan or line with foil in a medium bowl combine melted butter and cocoa and stir until cocoa is dissolved add sugar and mix well add eggs one at a time and stir until well combined stir in vanilla  flour and salt until you no longer see any flour fold in whatever floats your boat  spread in pan and bake for approximately 25 minutes do not overbake  your brownies will come out dry adjust time  temp accordingly for your oven if you do the knife  toothpick test  it should come out with moist crumbs  not clean cool completely before cutting into squares for vegetarian omit the marshmallows for double recipe  bake in 9x12 pan and add 5 minutes to baking time
