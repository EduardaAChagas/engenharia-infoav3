preheat oven to 350f brown ground beef until all pink is gone drain any grease put ground beef in the bottom of a greased 13x9x2inch pan spread cream of mushroom soup on top of ground beef cover with grated cheese top with tater tots cover with foil and bake covered 45 minutes uncover and bake for 15 more minutes
