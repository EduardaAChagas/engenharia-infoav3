preheat oven to 300f lightly grease and flour a 10 inch bundt pan cream shortening  butter and sugar until light and fluffy with an electric mixer add eggs one at a time  beating well after each addition beat in almond extract in another bowl  combine baking powder and flour stir into creamed mixture alternately with the milk  starting and ending with flour pour batter into prepared pan bake in the preheated oven for 1 to 1 1  2 hours  or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean allow cake to cool in pan for 10 minutes  then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely
