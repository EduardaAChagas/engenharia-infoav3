preheat oven to 350 degrees butter 2 loaf pans pour the lemon juice into the milk and stir until the milk is curdled set aside beat together the butter  sugar and vanilla until creamy add the eggs  mixing thoroughly sift together the flour  baking soda and salt add this to the buttersugar mixture alternately with the curdled milk stir in the bananas and nuts pour the batter into the prepared loaf pans and bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes  or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean
from the green thumb harvest cookbook. this won first prize in 1889 at the wabash indiana county fair by her greatgrandmother according to the woman who submitted it. this makes 2 loaves.
