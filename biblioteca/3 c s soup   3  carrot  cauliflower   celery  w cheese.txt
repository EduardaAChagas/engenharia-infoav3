in large stockpot add carrots  celery and cauliflower to 2 quarts water add margarine  salt cook 20 to 25 minutes on low heat  covered take off heat  add evaporated milk  two cups of milk and optional frozen peas put back on burner and heat on low and stir until warm and hot to serve do not boil ladle in serving bowls  pass the cheese if they want a soup topper serve with triscuits or a whole wheat cracker this is a very rich soup if you want to add more water  feel free
this is a chunked full vegetable soup with lots of vitamins. made for my special kids who had usually never tasted a fresh vegetable before. there are no onions garlic or peppers in any of the soups.
