in a large saucepan  bring the vegetable broth to a boil add the chopped broccoli  garlic and ginger reduce the heat to low and cover steam for about 5 minutes or until the broccoli is tender remove from the heat after the broccoli has cooled slightly  puree it in a food processor or blender  or use a hand immersion blender return it to the sauce pan add the soy milk or skim milk and soy sauce warm over low heat serve sprinkled with chopped red onion for vegetarian  vegan use vegetable broth for vegan use soy milk
this is from dr. weil.  it makes a nice change from a heavy creamy broccoli soup and for 99 calories per serving you can easily have 2 bowls  takes just a few minutes to make too.
