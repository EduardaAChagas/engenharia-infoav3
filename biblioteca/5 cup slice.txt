preheat oven to 200c  400f grease and line a 20 x 30 cm lamington  sponge roll tin with baking paper make sure the paper extends up and over the sides sprinkle the pan evenly with sultana  chocolate bits  peanuts and coconut drizzle the condensed milk over the top cover with a piece of greased foil and bake in the oven for 20 minutes reduce heat to 180c  350f  remove the foil and bake for about 15 minutes until golden and firm remove from oven and cool completely in the pan drizzle with the melted chocolate and allow to set remove and slice to serve
