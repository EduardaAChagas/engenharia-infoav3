combine first 3 ingredients in small saucepan stir over low until melted and smooth spread onto the bottom of lightly greased 9 x 13inch pan lined with aluminum foil put pan in the freezer while working on next layer for filling layer  heat butter in heavy saucepan over medium heat until melted add the sugar and evaporated milk bring to boil and stir constantly for 5 minutes remove from heat and stir in the marshmallow fluff  vanilla extract and peanut butter when smooth  mix in peanuts spread over first layer place back in freezer as you make caramel layer unwrap caramels and place in saucepan with whipping cream stir over low heat until melted and smooth spread over filling layer  freezing again for icing  repeat the first layer with milk chocolate  butterscotch chips  and peanut butter spread over caramel layer and then refrigerate one hour lift candy out of the pan using foil and cut into squares
