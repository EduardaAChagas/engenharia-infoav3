preheat oven to 350 degrees beat butter and sugars together on medium speed until fluffy add eggs and vanilla  mix well in a seperate bowl  whisk flour  soda  spices and salt until well combined mix flour mixture into the butter mixture stir in oats and raisins drop by heaping tbsp onto ungreased cookie sheet bake for 10 to 12 minutes cool slightly then remove from cookie sheet
this is adapted from the quaker oats vanishing oatmeal cookie recipe.  i love oatmeal cookies but most are too bland for me. my family gobble these up almost as soon as they come out of the oven. i use a small general purpose ice cream scoop to make uniformly sized cookies.enjoy
