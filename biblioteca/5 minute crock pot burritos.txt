rub southwest seasoning on both sides of pork and place in slow cooker pour 2 cups salsa over pork  cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours using two forks  shred pork meanwhile  wrap tortillas in aluminum foil and heat in a 350degree oven for 20 minutes or until heated through spoon pork mixture onto center of each tortilla and top with additional salsa  cheese and cilantro broil 1 minute or until cheese is bubbly roll up like a burrito to eat serve with black beans and spanish rice
that is 5 minutes after it has been in the slow cooker all day.  this recipe is by renae woods.
