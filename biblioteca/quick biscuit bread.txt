preheat oven to 400 degrees lightly grease a cookie sheet remove the buiscuits from the can and separate place one buiscuit in the center and place the remaining buiscuit around it slightly overlapping now flatten them out with your fingers into a 10 inch circle brush with the olive oil sprinkle with the remaining ingredients you can use any kind of cheese you have on hand chili powder and monterey jack and some chopped chillies give you a great texmex bread bake about 15 minute until edges are nice and brown pull apart to serve
this is a wonderful quick bread to make as an acompaniment to most any dish. it is very versatile and delicious 1
