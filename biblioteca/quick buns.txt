grease or parchment paper 1 or 2 cookie sheets  depending on the size dissolve sugar in warm water sprinkle yeast over liquid combine milk  salt  and butter in saucepan and heat until warm add yeast and flour work and knead dough for about 10 minutes cover and let rise in warm place for about 30 minutes turn on floured surface and pat to 3  4 thickness cut with round cookie cutter and place on prepared sheets let rise in warm place for about 45 minutes or until double in size i usually put some flour in a hand sieve and sprinkle the tops before baking bake at 375 degrees for 1520 minutes
these buns are light and wonderful.  in just over 2 hrs from start to finish.  this recipe is from the canadian mennonite cookbook.
