chop mushrooms finely spray a large nonstick pot with cooking spray and place over medium heat add 1  2 cup of the chopped mushrooms and cook until tender remove to a bowl add the butter to the pot  along with the garlic and onions cook for 3 minutes  stirring add the mushrooms and cook  stirring  until tender add the mushroom broth  white beans  and seasonings bring to a boil  reduce heat  cover  and simmer for about 810 minutes  letting the flavors cook together remove from heat and let cool slightly with an immersion blender or a food processor  puree the soup if you have reserved mushroom pieces  stir them back into the soup as you reheat over medium garnish with parmesan cheese if desired
