preheat oven to 350 degrees spray a 2 quart baking dish with cooking spray  set aside in a large bowl mix together broccoli  soup  one cup of cheese  garlic powder  pepper  salt  milk  1 cup of french onions  and soy sauce pour into baking dish  sprinkle remaining cheese over top bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is lightly browned sprinkle with rest of french fried onions and bake until onions are browned and cheese is bubbly  about 10 more minutes
