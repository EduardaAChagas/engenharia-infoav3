brown ground beef in large pot add chopped onions to ground beef when almost brown and sautee until wilted add all other ingredients add kidney beans if you like beans in your chili cook in slow cooker on high for 23 hours or 68 hours on low serve with cold clean lettuce and shredded cheese
this modified version of moms chili was a hit at our 2004 christmas party. we made an extra large pot to have some left to freeze but it never made it to the freezer. it was a favorite by all. perfect for any cold and rainy day. you wont find this one in a cookbook. it is truly an original.
