cut bacon slices crossways into 1  2 inch pieces fry bacon in a sauce pan until just crisp remove bacon from pan and let drain on paper towels pour off all but a couple teaspoons of bacon fat add onion to pan and cook just until translucent and soft if using whole tomatoes  cut them up using a sharp knife while theyre still in the can add tomatoes to the pan along with the drained can of green beans add bacon and heat until all ingredients are thoroughly heated serve in small bowls because its kind of soupy
