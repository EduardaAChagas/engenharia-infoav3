preheat the oven to 375 degrees f grease a 9x9inch baking dish mix tomato sauce  water  taco seasoning mix  and chili powder together in a saucepan bring to a simmer over medium heat heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat and brown chicken tenderloins on both sides  about 5 minutes per side pour tomato sauce mixture over the chicken  bring to a simmer  and cook over mediumlow heat until chicken tenderloins are no longer pink inside  about 8 minutes transfer chicken to a bowl and shred return shredded chicken to the sauce mix in black beans and cream cheese until thoroughly combined pour chicken mixture into prepared baking dish top with shredded mexican cheese whisk corn bread mix  egg  and milk in a bowl  and spoon the batter over the chicken mix bake in the preheated oven until the casserole is bubbling and the corn bread topping is browned and set  about 30 minutes
