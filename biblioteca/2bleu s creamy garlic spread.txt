preheat oven to 300f leaving the paper on  cut off the top of the the garlic bulbs horizontally  just enough to expose the cloves place the garlic cutside up onto a sheet of aluminum foil and drizzle top with olive oil sprinkle with salt and pepper seal up the bulb in the foil  place in a baking dish and bake for 1 hour allow garlic to cool  then squeeze the cloves from their peel into a clean bowl using the tines of a fork  mash the cloves and mix with the mayonnaise and horseradish  mixing well will keep for a week or two in the fridge
this is a great condiment perfect for roast beef or warm grilled chicken... at the table or on a sandwich  this keeps about a month in your fridge.
