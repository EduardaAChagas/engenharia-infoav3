in a large skillet  heat the water over medium heat  and add the tvp  stirring well allow the tvp to reconstitute for 23 minutes add oil and soy sauce  then peppers and taco seasoning  stirring well allow to cook for another 35 minutes  stirring frequently mix in salsa and remove from heat serve wrapped in a flour tortilla or hard taco shells with your choice of toppings
a jolinda hackett recipe.his recipe uses storebought salsa and taco seasoning to make a superquick vegetarian taco filling from tvp. the tvp has a texture similar to shredded chicken when cooked. wrap in a flour tortilla or stuff in a hard shell with lettuce tomatoes avocado and green onions.
