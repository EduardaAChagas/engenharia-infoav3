preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit melt butter pour into the bottom of a 9 by 12 baking pan pour graham cracker crumbs over butter pat down add the chocolate chips  butterscotch chips  pecans  and coconut in the order listed  sprinkling in an even layer for the final layer  pour the can of sweetened condensed milk over the entire top bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 25 to 30 minutes until top is nice and and golden cool completely and cut into bars they are not quite 9x12 but something like 11 x 2inches deep they work wonderfully
