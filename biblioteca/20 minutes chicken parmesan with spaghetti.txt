flatten chicken to even thickness using palm of hand or meat mallet dip chicken in egg  then coat with crumbs in 10inch skillet over medium heat  heat margarine and cook chicken until brown on both sides add spaghetti sauce reduce heat to low and cover  simmer for 10 minutes sprinkle with cheeses and parsley cover and allow to simmer 5 more minutes or until cheese melts serve over hot spaghetti
