preheat oven to 350 degrees f grease and flour 3 cake pans  using an electric mixer  cream butter until fluffy add sugar and continue to cream well for 6 to 8 minutes add eggs  1 at a time  beating well after each addition add flour and milk alternately to creamed mixture  beginning and ending with flour add vanilla and continue to beat until just mixed divide batter equally among prepared pans level batter in each pan by holding pan 3 or 4 inches above counter  then dropping it flat onto counter do this several times to release air bubbles and assure you of a more level cake bake for 25 to 30 minutes or until done cool in pans 5 to 10 minutes invert cakes onto cooling racks cool completely and spread cake layers with your favorite frosting to make a 3layer cake
