cook macaroni according to package directions  drain and place in large mixing bowl saute onion  mushrooms and bacon in olive oil until carmelized add wine and reduce let cool and stir this mixture into cooked macaroni prepare white sauce by melting butter and adding flour  cook until bubbly  then slowly add milk at medium low heat  stirring constantly  cook until velvety and thickened add cheese and stir until completely melted and smooth add sauce to macaroni  stir well and season with parsley  salt and pepper and chili flake  if using spoon into ungreased baking dish  spread breadcrumbs evenly over surface  bake at 350 f for about 30 minutes
this is a great way to use up leftover bits of cheese and other things in the fridge.  simple ingredients delicious and quick to prepare.  make ahead and pop into the oven for dinner great for the freezer too.
