heat oven to 300 degrees combine oats  nuts  salt  cinnamon and flax in a large bowl and set aside combine oil  honey and vanilla in another  smaller bowl mix wet ingredients into dry  you may need to use your hands then spread the mixture onto two cookie sheets lined with foil or parchment bake 10 minutes then remove from oven and stir bake an additional 10 minutes or until slightly golden remove from oven and allow to cool completely when cooled  transfer into an airtight container for storage
this is a super easy granola recipe. it isnt overly sweet and is a real crowdpleaser. it also makes your house smell fabulous for hours after you bake it. i normally add about 2 tbsp. wheat germ and a tsp. brewers yeast to my dry ingredients  for nutritional value.
