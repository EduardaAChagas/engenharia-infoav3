slice carrots and steam  set aside saute parsley and garlic for 1 minute in an oiled pan add carrots and veggie broth simmer uncovered for 5 minutes add tofu  vegan cream cheese  and lemon juice stir well cook over medium heat stirring constantly until cheese starts to melt stir in nutmeg and nutritional yeast remove from heat keep warm place 3 lasagna noodles in bottom of 11x7 baking dish layer carrot mixture  noodles  tomato sauce and spinach until done top with spaghetti sauce cover and bake 15 minutes at 375 degrees uncover and bake an additional 30 minutes until thoroughly heated
