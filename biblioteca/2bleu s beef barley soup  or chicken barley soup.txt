preparing barley like all grains  before cooking barley  rinse it thoroughly under running water and then remove any dirt or debris that you may find in a large dutch oven heat oil over medium heat  add meat and garlic cook until meat is browned drain if necessary stir in remaining ingredients bring to a boil  reduce heat and simmer for 11 1  2 hours  stirring occasionally until barley and carrots are tender and fully cooked season to taste with additional salt  black pepper and serve pearl barley takes a minimum of 1 hour to cook  whereas hulled barley takes 1 5 hours or more
