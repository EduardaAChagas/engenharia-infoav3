in the peak of tomato season  chill 1 very large or 2 medium size tomatoes that have been vineripened and have plenty juice take 2 slices of bread and coat them with 1  4 inch good mayonnaise add layer of tomatoes cut 1  4 inch thick salt  pepper to taste add another slice of tomatoes and salt and pepper to taste again add the other piece of bread on top of this roll up your sleeves and start eating over the kitchen sink while the juice runs down your elbows
i got this recipe from an old georgia boy that was transplanted in tennessee he is 72 and says he grew up on this sandwich.
