heat a medium nonstick skillet over high heat and coat with cooking spray pour half of the eggs into the pan  swirling to coat the bottom  and let cook until set  about 1 minute remove the skillet from the heat flip the eggs and cook for 1 minute more transfer the egg tortilla to a cutting board and repeat the process with the cooking spray and the remaining egg roll up each egg tortilla and slice crosswise into 1  4 inch thick strips in a large skillet  heat the sesame oil over high heat add the shrimp  chorizo and onion and cook  stirring  until the shrimp is just opaque  1  2 minutes mix in the rice  soy sauce and sherry cook until heated through stir in the egg strips and top with cilantro
