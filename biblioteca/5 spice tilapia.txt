sprinkle both sides of tilapia fillets with 5spice powder combine soy sauce and brown sugar in a small bowl heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over mediumhigh heat add the tilapia and cook until the outer edges are opaque  about 2 minutes reduce heat to medium  turn the fish over  stir the soy mixture and pour into the pan bring the sauce to a boil and cook until the fish is cooked through and the sauce has thickened slightly  about 2 minutes more add scallions and remove from the heat serve the fish drizzled with the sauce
