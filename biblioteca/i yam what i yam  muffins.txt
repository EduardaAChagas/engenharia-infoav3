preheat oven to 375 degrees spray muffin tin with nonstick cooking spray and set aside place the raisins in a small bowl and pour the 1  4 cup boiling water over them cover and set aside while preparing batter combine the flours  cocoa  baking powder  cinnamon  cloves  baking soda  and salt lightly beat eggs with sorghum  oil  mashed yams and orange juice add the egg mixture and buttermilk alternately to the dry ingredients  stir until well blended drain the raisins and add to batter along with orange peel spoon batter into prepared muffin tins  sprinkle 1  4 to 1  2 tsp of granulated sugar over each muffin bake in preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes
these muffins may have slightly different ingredients but the end result is a wonderful rich moist muffin. the sugar sprinkled on top before cooking gives a slightly sweet crunch and cracked glazed appearance.
