heat butter and oil in large nonstick skillet over mediumhigh heat add all but 2 tablespoons green onions saut 30 seconds add rice and saut until heated through  about 4 minutes add peas  lemon juice  zest  parsley  and salt and pepper saut 2 minutes to blend flavors garnish with remaining green onions and optional chopped walnuts and serve immediately
this side dish is a light refreshing and a wonderful accompiniment to broiled fish or meat. it is also a great use of leftovers. you can vary the nuts from walnuts to pecans pine nuts or even sunflower seeds.
