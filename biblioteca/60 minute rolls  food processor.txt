combine yeast  1 tablespoon sugar  and warm water in a 2cup liquid measuring cup let stand 5 minutes add milk to yeast mixture and stir in a food processor  combine flour  salt  remaining 2 tablespoons sugar  and butter or margarine pulse until thoroughly incorporated with food processor running  slowly drizzle yeast mixture through feed tube until ball forms run machine for about 2 minutes to knead dough thoroughly remove dough from processor and place in a well greased bowl  turning to coat top cover and let stand in a warm place for 15 minutes punch down dough divide dough in half shape one portion of dough into 12 balls place in a greased 8 or 9inch square pan repeat with remaining half of dough cover pans and let rise in a warm place for 15 minutes bake at 425f for 1015 minutes or until rolls are golden brown
these yeasty rolls are great when you want dinner rolls and you are short on time.
