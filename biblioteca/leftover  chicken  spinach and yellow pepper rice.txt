place the stock in a small saucepan and bring to simmering point meanwhile  heat the olive oil in a large saucepan add the yellow pepper and fry  stirring often  until the pepper has softened but not browned add the rice and continue cooking and stirring for 12 minutes  until the rice is translucent and shiny pour the wine into the pan of rice and simmer until it has almost evaporated add a ladle of the hot stock to the rice and cook  stirring constantly  over a low heat until the liquid has been absorbed continue adding the stock to the rice a little at a time and stirring until the liquid has been absorbed  for about 17 minutes when the rice is almost done  add the chicken and the spinach leaves and stir to mix evenly allow to heat through for 23 minutes  then adjust the seasoning to taste with salt and pepper and serve hot
