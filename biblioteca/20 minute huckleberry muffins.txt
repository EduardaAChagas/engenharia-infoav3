preheat oven to 400f grease a standard 12muffin pan or line with paper cups thoroughly mix the flour  baking powder  salt and huckleberries together in a large bowl set aside in another bowl  whisk together eggs  milk or cream  sugar  butter  and vanilla add the wet ingredients to the dry and mix together with a few light strokes just until just blended do not overmix the batter should not be smooth divide the batter among the muffin cups bake for 15 minutes  or until a toothpick inserted in 1 or 2 of the muffins comes out clean let cool for a few minutes  and serve warm
